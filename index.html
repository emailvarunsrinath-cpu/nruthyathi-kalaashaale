About Us

Nruthyathi Kalaashaale, founded by Smt. Anusha Varun, is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Bharatanatyam.

Over the years, the institution has trained hundreds of students and organized multiple annual festivals, Samaagama, showcasing classical dance excellence.
