About Us

Nruthyathi Kalaashaale (R.) is a premier Bharathanatyam dance institute and art center in Mysuru, dedicated to preserving and spreading the cultural heritage of classical dance. Under the guidance of Smt. Anusha Varun, the institution nurtures young talents and provides a platform to learn, practice, and perform Bharathanatyam in its traditional and contemporary forms.
